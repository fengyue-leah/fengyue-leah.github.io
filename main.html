Hi! I'm a third-year PhD student in Computer Science at University College London (UCL) advised by Prof. Emine Yilmaz. Prior to that, I obtained M.Sc. from Chinese Academy of Sciences and B.Sc. from Harbin Institute of Technology. I also spent wonderful time doing research at Amazon, ByteDance, Huawei, and Baidu.

My research interests lie in natural language processing and information retrieval, with an emphasis on conversational artiﬁcial intelligence, recommender systems, and large language models.


Feel free to reach me at yue.feng.20@ucl.ac.uk.
